About Sarah J. Maas

Sarah J. Maas is the #1 New York Times and internationally bestselling
author of the Court of Thorns and Roses and the Crescent City series,
as well as the Throne of Glass series. Her books are published in more
than thirty-seven languages. A New York native, Sarah lives in Pennsylvania
with her husband, son, and dog.
